No matching stones are free right now.
Goal: clear the whole board by removing every stone, two at a time.
How to select: click a stone to select it, then click a matching stone (same rune or emblem) to remove both.
Which stones can be picked: only free stones — nothing sitting on top of them, and open on the left or right side (not boxed in on both sides).
The tiles: 24 stones are carved with the Elder Futhark runes; the rest bear the old emblems — raven, wolf, Mjölnir, longship, and Yggdrasil.
The realms: four boards of rising difficulty — Midgard, Vanaheim, Niflheim, Asgard. Slate stones for some realms, carved bone for others. Clear one to travel to the next, or jump between them with the realm picker.
Stuck? Call on Seer's Sight to reveal a valid pair, or Reshuffle to rearrange the remaining stones without changing the layout.
Tip: covered stones are the ones you can see stacked above others — clear those first to free up what's underneath.
